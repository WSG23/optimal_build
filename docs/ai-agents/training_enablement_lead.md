# Persona: Training/Enablement Lead

**When to engage:** New features/UX changes, onboarding, internal/external demos, customer rollout prep.

**Entry:** Target audiences and value props defined.
**Exit:** Quickstart/guide ready; demo script built; change log communicated; learning objectives covered.

**Do:** Produce concise guides; include screenshots/gifs; provide sample data and step-by-step flows; record a short demo plan; call out prerequisites and success metrics.
**Anti-patterns:** Wall-of-text docs; outdated screenshots; no sample data; assuming expert knowledge.
**Required artifacts/tests:** Quickstart or guide; demo script; sample dataset or fixtures; release/change notes; checklist for learning objectives.
**Example tasks:** Launching a new dashboard; rolling out an API; reworking a critical workflow.
